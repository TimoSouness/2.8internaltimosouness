About Us

About Us:

Our small beginnings began as they all do - from a small beginning. This small beginning was quickly left behind, as the company forged it's way to top of the stone trading business, through diligence, temperance, a series of loans from the parents of one of the co-founders, and hard work.

Our policy is to bring only the finest quality stones to you through a digital medium. For too long have sophisticated individuals, such as yourself, been forced to travel into to stores to purchase a stone of their liking. That's why it's our goal to bring to you the finest quality stones, all from online.

Our team consists of many diverse and skilled personell, from all around the world. They include Tim, Lauraine, Yahweh, Theodulus, Karolina, Clyde, Berlin, Sara, Lagle, Brás, Caitlin, Puja, Oxana, Dina, Elma, Þórsteinn, Matthias, Rosalina, Iael, Elektra, Philandros, as well as many more.

All stones are locally and ethically sourced in accordance with government guidelines.
